About TerraCognita

We give you useful data in an accessible format

The data is out there. In the wild. It is accessible for people who can spend time to get it and make sense of it.

We do that for you. We select the best sources of public data, crunching it and providing it in a the de facto format for geographical data - GeoJSON

Here are some of the data categories we currently support.
  • Air quality information
  • Police crime reports
  • Housing - renting/buying
  • Restaurants hygiene rating
  • Schools

Nike Running

Integration with running apps

Project Description

Since the beginning of TerraCognita we have an ambition - make life better.

We want to integrate our product with apps for running and excersising.

Those 3rd party apps can use information from us to improve the experience of their customers. As an example, if you are running towards an area with a high concentration of crimes(we can tell you that :) ), Nike Running can notify you about this using their voice notifications. Pretty cool, huh? :)
